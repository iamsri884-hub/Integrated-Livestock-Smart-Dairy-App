# Integrated Livestock & Smart Dairy Management App

A modern mobile application designed to manage the complete livestock and dairy business lifecycle, especially for rural and semi-urban environments.

## Key Features
- Livestock buy & sell using unique IDs
- Milk production tracking (morning/evening)
- Milk sales to companies & sweet shops
- Dairy product management (curd, butter, ghee, paneer)
- Vaikol (fodder) procurement management
- Market price tracking & price alerts
- Cow health & vaccination reminders
- Simple analytics & profit calculation
- Offline milk entry with later sync
- Student part-time selling & deals module

## User Roles
- Admin
- Farmer/User
- Student (Part-time seller)

## Tech Stack (Planned)
- Frontend: Flutter
- Backend: Node.js / PHP
- Database: MySQL
